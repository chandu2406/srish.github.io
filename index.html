Srishti Sethi SrishAkaTux

Hi, born and brought up in a desert town of Rajasthan, India, I'm a Masters student at MIT Media Lab with Lifelong Kindergarten research group advised by Mitchel Resnick. Currently, I am exploring how online platforms for learning can be made more peer-led, participatory and accessible to diverse populations.

Before coming to the Media Lab, I was working as a software developer for a start up organization ( VCL) in Bangalore. While in undergrad, I was involved with an open source community, GNOME. As a Student Contractor at Google, I developed an engaging user-experience by extending GCompris to kids to help them explore a variety of activities theming braille.

My academics focus aside, I harbor a deep interest for music and ping-pong. I love to blog about my experiences with life, technology and travel. To allure myself at times, I read spiritual books, do photography and cook Rajma Chawal.
